Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients List:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps to Cook:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat a pan over medium heat and add pancetta. Cook until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add it to the pan with pancetta, removing from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture, allowing the heat of the pasta to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra cheese and parsley if desired.
