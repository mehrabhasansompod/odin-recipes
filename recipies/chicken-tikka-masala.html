Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

This Chicken Tikka Masala features marinated chicken in a rich and creamy tomato sauce, served with rice.

Ingredients List:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 200g plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala paste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to Cook:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt and tikka masala paste. Add chicken and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© chopped onion until translucent.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in canned tomatoes and coconut milk, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh coriander before serving.
