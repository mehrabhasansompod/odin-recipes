Moussaka

Description

This moussaka tastes great! Layers of sliced eggplant are baked in a ground beef sauce and smothered in a creamy white béchamel sauce in this classic Greek dish.

Ingredients List:

  • 3 eggplants, peeled and cut lengthwise into 1/2 inch thick slices
  • salt to taste
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon fines herbs
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg, divided
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • ½ cup red wine
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 4 cups milk
  • ½ cup butter
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ground white pepper, to taste
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to Cook:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the eggplant:
    1. Slice the eggplant into 1/2 inch thick rounds.
    2. Sprinkle salt on the slices and let them sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture.
    3. Rinse the eggplant slices under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels.
    4. Heat olive oil in a pan and fry the eggplant slices until golden brown on both sides. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce:
    1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add chopped onions and garlic; sauté until soft.
    3. Add ground meat (beef or lamb) and cook until browned.
    4. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine, and spices (cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and pepper).
    5. Let the sauce simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Prepare the béchamel sauce:
    1. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    2. Add flour and whisk for about 2 minutes until the mixture is lightly browned.
    3. Gradually add milk while whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
    4. Cook until the sauce thickens, then remove from heat and stir in egg yolks and cheese.
    5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Assemble the moussaka:
    1. In a baking dish, layer half of the fried eggplant slices.
    2. Spread the meat sauce evenly over the eggplant.
    3. Layer the remaining eggplant slices on top of the meat sauce.
    4. Pour the béchamel sauce over the top layer of eggplant, spreading it evenly.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  7. Allow the moussaka to cool for at least 30 minutes before serving to help it set.
  8. Cut into squares and serve warm, garnished with parsley if desired.
