Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate Chip Cookies are delicious, soft, and chewy treats filled with chocolate chips, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 150g unsalted butter, softened
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 200g chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps to Cook:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar.
  3. Add egg and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  4. In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the wet mixture.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
